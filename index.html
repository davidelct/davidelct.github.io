I am a junior researcher in the INTERACT group at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya, Barcelona. Together with my doctoral advisors Ariadna Quattoni and Xavier Carreras, I conduct research in Interactive Machine Learning, with a focus on Natural Language Processing tasks including Semantic Parsing and Sequence Classification.

I am also currently completing a Master of Research in Artificial Intelligence at the Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona. My thesis work revolves around Emergent Multi-Agent Communication, and I am supervised by Roberto Dessì and Marco Baroni. Previously, I obtained a Master of Science in Computer Science at Imperial College, London, where I worked on Compositional Generalization of Modular Neural Networks, supervised by Pranava Madhyasta and Josiah Wang.
